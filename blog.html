ITIII Blog Entries

Welcome to my ITIII Blog, where I share my insights and learnings from the course.

Lesson 1: Introduction to Computing

An overview of computing concepts and systems.

My Learnings:

In this lesson, I learned about the basics of computing, including the different types of computers and how they function. I discovered the significance of hardware and software in the computing process.

Lesson 2: Professions & Career in Computing

Exploring different careers and paths in the IT field.

My Learnings:

This lesson helped me understand the various career opportunities in the IT industry. I learned about different job roles, including software developer, systems administrator, and data scientist.

Lesson 3: Information Processing Cycle

Understanding the stages of processing data in a computer system.

My Learnings:

This lesson focused on the stages of data processing, from input to processing, storage, and output. I gained a deeper understanding of how data flows through a system and how it is processed efficiently.

Lesson 4: Introduction to Computer System

A deep dive into the hardware and software components of a computer.

My Learnings:

In this lesson, I explored the different components of a computer system, including the CPU, RAM, and hard drive. I also learned how software interacts with hardware to perform tasks.

My Favorite Lesson

After reflecting on all the lessons, I found that Lesson 3: Information Processing Cycle was the most insightful for me. I enjoyed learning how data flows through the system and the stages of processing it.
